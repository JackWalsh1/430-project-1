Don't get sucked in!

Foundational Elements

Genre: Card / Board, Symmetric, 1v1
Platform: Any HTML5 Equipped Browser
Story: None, unless you consider a game of strategy a story.
Aesthetics: Minimalistic and clean. The focus is on the game, so having simple shapes / a 8-color palette helps.
Gameplay: Selecting tiles and reading your opponent. The rules are written out in each game through a tutorial. .

Black Hole

Black Hole is a tile placement game where each player places tiles from 1-10 on a board, attempting to have the lowest numerical value of all the tiles touching the one unfilled tile (aka the Black Hole). It could easily be a bunch of DIVs in a triange that changes based on what's clicked.

Why This?

The game is simple enough to produce yet has multiple elements to tackle - understanding the DOM fully, AI "design", 2-player toggles, multiple events linking to the same place, and a real first "game" that I've played before.
